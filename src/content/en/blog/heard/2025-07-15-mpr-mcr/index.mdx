We will share what we heard about IOT from Hanselman's "The Computer Stuff They Didn't Teach You" series.

There are two types of circuits called **microprocessor** and **microcontroller**. A **microprocessor** is generally what we call a small computer brain. You can run operating systems on it. However, a **microcontroller** is a circuit designed for specific technologies. For example, they are found in things we use every day like microwave ovens and washing machines.
